## 后验阿尔法分解

$$
r_p=\beta_p*r_b+\alpha_p+\epsilon
$$

$$
\beta_p= {Cov(r_p,r_b)\over Var(r_b)}
$$

$$
\omega_p=\sqrt{\sigma_p^2-\beta_p^2\sigma_b^2}
$$

$$
IR={\alpha \over \omega}
$$
